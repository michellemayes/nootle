# Design Context

## Target Audience
Tech professionals who want to download Nootle to record their meetings — engineers, PMs, designers in fast-moving teams.

## Brand Personality
Playful and friendly, but professional. The name "Nootle" is casual; the product is serious about privacy and productivity.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Playful, approachable, confident. Not corporate, not neon-flashy.
- **Palette**: Purple accent (#8b5cf6) on cool-tinted dark neutrals. No tri-color neon (mint/blue/magenta).
- **Typography**: Outfit (display) + DM Sans (body). Clean, modern, readable.
- **Motion**: Subtle, purposeful scroll-triggered fades. No gratuitous glow or blob animations.
- **Layout**: Centered sections are fine for a landing page, but vary rhythm and spacing.

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- Neon accents on cold dark backgrounds
- Gradient text on headings
- Gradient borders
- Dot-grid backgrounds
- Glowing colored blobs
- Identical card grids with icon + title + description
- Triple-color accent palettes (mint/blue/magenta)
